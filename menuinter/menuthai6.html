GAMING SERVICE: ETHEREAL CRYSTAL

Concept & Ideology The design concept centers on "Seamless Virtual Transaction," aiming to create a trustworthy and professional platform for gamers. By utilizing the iconic aesthetic of Genshin Impact—specifically the bright, ethereal blue of Genesis Crystals—the design establishes an immediate connection with the target audience, promoting a fast and secure top-up experience.

Wide-Angle Panoramic Layout Utilizing a 16:9 cinematic ratio, the design provides an expansive canvas that prevents the information from feeling cluttered. The "Floating Grid" arrangement allows the various crystal packages to be spread out evenly, giving the viewer a clear, organized view of all available options at a single glance.

Hierarchical Price Callouts The design employs "Visual Anchoring" by placing the most popular package (980+110 Crystals) in the center with a "ยอดนิยม" (Popular) tag. Each price point is rendered in a large, bold dark-blue font, creating a strong contrast against the light background to ensure that the cost is the most prominent information for the user.

Cyan-to-White Gradient Palette The color scheme uses a soft gradient of cyan and sky blue, reflecting the "In-game Sky" theme of Teyvat. This bright palette creates an airy, trustworthy feel, while the pops of vibrant blue from the Genesis Crystal icons add energy and focus to the specific product offerings.

Character & Environmental Integration To add personality and warmth, chibi-style game characters and a "Blessing of the Welkin Moon" card are placed at the bottom center. This, combined with the realistic grass foreground, adds a layer of "Environmental Storytelling," making the service feel like a friendly extension of the game world itself.

Transparent Iconography The design utilizes high-quality transparent PNGs for the crystals, allowing the soft background light to pass through them. This creates a "Luminous Effect," making the virtual currency look valuable and enticing, mirroring the high-quality graphical standard of the game.

Functional Trust Footer The bottom section is reserved for a dark green "Information Strip," containing contact details and trust indicators like "Safe & Secure." This strategic placement ensures that while the top is focused on sales, the bottom provides the necessary functional information and social proof to convert a viewer into a customer.

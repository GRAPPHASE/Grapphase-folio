ISAAN FOOD: RUSTIC ZEST

Concept & Ideology The design captures the "Soul of the Northeast," focusing on the raw, spicy, and vibrant nature of Isaan cuisine. It uses a "Market Style" aesthetic to evoke a sense of authenticity and local charm, positioning the dishes as bold, flavorful comfort food that is deeply rooted in Thai street culture.

Torn Paper Texture & Contrast The layout is uniquely divided by a "Torn Paper" vertical edge, separating the product zone from the branding zone. This tactile element adds a handcrafted, rustic feel to the poster, mimicking the informal and energetic vibe of a traditional Thai open-air eatery.

Dynamic Script & Vertical Typography The word "เมนู" (Menu) is arranged vertically in a bold, brush-style Thai script. This vertical orientation breaks traditional reading patterns, creating a stylish, modern look while the handwritten style reinforces the "Home-cooked" and personal touch of the brand.

Fiery Crimson & Botanical Background The deep crimson background represents the "Heat" of the chilies, a core ingredient in Isaan food. This is layered with subtle line-art illustrations of vegetables and herbs, adding textural depth without distracting from the main food photography.

Tiered Vertical Presentation The three signature dishes—Som Tum, Larb Moo, and Kor Moo Yang—are stacked vertically on the left. This "Progressive Layout" allows each dish to have its own clear space, making the variety of the menu easy to navigate for the customer.

Brush-Stroke Price Tagging Each dish is accompanied by a white "Brush-Stroke" label for its name and price (50.-). The organic shape of these labels creates a cohesive look with the torn-paper background, ensuring the pricing information feels integrated into the art rather than just an overlay.

Illustrative Brand Identity The "Isaan Food" logo, featuring a traditional mortar and pestle (Krok), is placed prominently on the white section. The green accents in the logo symbolize the fresh lime and herbs, providing a visual balance to the red tones and emphasizing the brand's commitment to "Fresh & Zesty" ingredients.

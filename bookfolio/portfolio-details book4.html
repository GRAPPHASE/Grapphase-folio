Eucerin Brochure Design: A dermatological brochure design created for Eucerin facial serums, emphasizing clinical efficacy, skin safety, and scientific credibility.

Design Concept: The brochure communicates skincare benefits through a medical-focused approach, translating dermatological research into clear, consumer-friendly information.

Visual Identity: A clean, clinical visual language aligned with Eucerin’s pharmaceutical heritage, using minimal compositions, soft gradients, and precise layouts to convey skin health and reliability.

Layout & Information Structure: Content is organized into clear sections highlighting key active ingredients, skin concerns, clinical test results, and usage instructions for easy understanding and professional presentation.

Typography: Highly legible sans-serif typography with structured hierarchy ensures medical-grade readability while maintaining a modern and approachable tone.

Purpose & Communication: The brochure is designed to build consumer confidence by presenting evidence-based skincare information, reinforcing Eucerin’s position as a trusted dermatological brand.

Lab of longevity

A creative sunscreen packaging design for 'CELESTIQUE' featuring a 'Multiverse Sunscreen' theme. Guided by the slogan 'Your Skin, Your Universe,' the design uses celestial and solar system graphics to represent comprehensive and advanced skin protection.

Our process

Strategy & Design Direction

Core Concept: Presenting the "Your Skin, Your Universe" identity through packaging that merges cosmic wonder with scientific innovation.

Aesthetic Direction: Premium minimalist style with high contrast, utilizing Pristine White as a base against deep cosmic tones for maximum shelf standout.

Visual Identity: A balanced blend of medical-grade reliability and a dreamy celestial atmosphere, featuring star and galaxy elements.

Key Highlights:
• Integration of a brand mascot to enhance memory and engagement
• Clean Sans-Serif typography reflecting professionalism and efficiency
• Matte finish aesthetics with special tactile accents to elevate the luxury experience

Functional Layout: Systematic organization of technical data and safety symbols, including multi-language instructions and international standard certifications.

Technical Specification Check: Confirming the final die-cut dimensions and physical material properties before commencing the design phase. Since the client provided only ough box dimensions, I opted for an elongated rectangular structure to accommodate the vertical form factor of the sunscreen tube.

Competitor & Market Research: Conducting a comprehensive analysis of competitors within the same price bracket. I researched various sunscreen brands and analyzed retail shelf displays to identify visual gaps, ensuring the final design effectively stands out from the competition.

Concept Development: Integrating the client's brief with analytical data to develop creative directions. The project was executed across three distinct color palettes as specified by the client’s brand guidelines.

Mockup Testing & Validation:
Applying the digital designs onto physical or 3D models to verify proportions, legibility, and overall aesthetic appeal.

GAMING SERVICE: CELESTIAL ESTEEM

Concept & Ideology The design core is inspired by "Divine Elegance," utilizing the high-tier 'Lauriel Esteem' skin to establish a sense of premium exclusivity. It aims to attract high-value players by reflecting the majestic and ethereal qualities of the game’s top-tier aesthetics, positioning the top-up service as a high-end, reliable platform.

Majestic Character Showcase By placing Lauriel in a centered, large-scale background, the design leverages "Hero Worship" psychology. The character's wings frame the entire composition, creating a natural visual boundary that directs the user's attention inward toward the central RoV logo and the price list.

Parallel Parallelogram Grid The price list is organized using sharp, slanted parallelogram shapes. This "Parallel Motion" design adds a sense of speed and modern edge, mirroring the fast-paced nature of MOBA gaming while keeping the data perfectly aligned for quick comparison.

Ethereal Blue & Gold Palette The color scheme revolves around "Celestial Blue" and "Divine White," which are the signature colors of the Lauriel Esteem skin. This palette communicates purity, trust, and high value, while the subtle gold accents in the character art add a touch of luxury to the overall service image.

Striking Price Comparison The design uses "Anchor Pricing" by showing the original price crossed out in red next to the new, discounted price in bold blue. This visual technique highlights the "Value for Money" aspect, making the savings immediately apparent and encouraging faster purchasing decisions.

Luminous Voucher Iconography The "Blue Vouchers" are rendered with a glowing, crystalline effect. By stacking them in groups that grow in size, the design provides a visual representation of "Wealth Accumulation," making the larger top-up bundles feel more substantial and rewarding to the customer.

High-End Brand Symmetry The entire layout follows a strict "Symmetrical Balance," with the "Muscle Game Top Up" logo on the left and the "24-Hour Service" icon on the right. This balance reflects the brand’s "Stability and Professionalism," ensuring the customer feels they are dealing with a well-established and secure service provider.

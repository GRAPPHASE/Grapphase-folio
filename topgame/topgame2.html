GAMING SERVICE: ARCANE VELOCITY

Concept & Ideology The design core focuses on "Dynamic Speed and Modernity," utilizing a Cyberpunk-inspired aesthetic to resonate with the League of Legends community. By blending high-energy neon tones with professional service layouts, it establishes a brand identity that is both exciting and trustworthy for the competitive gaming market.

Immersive Character Background The layout utilizes a high-quality game splash art as a background, creating an immediate emotional connection with players. By using a "Large-Scale Character" focal point, the design feels like an official extension of the game world, which increases consumer confidence in the service.

Vibrant Neon Visual Hierarchy The design employs a "Neon Glow" effect on key information. The use of bright cyan for price tags against a deep purple background creates a high-contrast focal point, ensuring that users can immediately identify the value of each RP package without visual fatigue.

Ultraviolet & Cyan Palette The color scheme is centered on a "Mystic Purple" base, symbolizing the arcane and magical themes of the game. This is balanced by "Electric Cyan" accents for the buttons and price labels, creating a modern, high-tech look that is synonymous with the digital gaming era.

Symmetric Grid Organization The RP packages are organized in a clean, 3-column symmetric grid. This "Modular Design" approach simplifies complex pricing data into digestible blocks, allowing the customer to compare different Riot Point (RP) bundles side-by-side efficiently.

Tiered Currency Iconography The design features progressive iconography—from small gold coin stacks to large treasure chests. This visual scaling reinforces the "Value Growth" concept, making the higher-priced tiers look more rewarding and physically substantial to the buyer.

24/7 Availability Branding A prominent "24 Service" icon with a glowing clock is placed at the top right. This strategic placement ensures that the service's biggest competitive advantage—round-the-clock availability—is one of the first things a customer notices, providing peace of mind for late-night gamers.

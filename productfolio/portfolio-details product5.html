Macaron Packaging Design

A packaging design for "Legno" macarons under the theme "A Taste of Nature’s Elegance." This concept highlights the delicate, handcrafted nature of the confectionery, using premium natural ingredients. The design focuses on the contrast between the vibrant colors of the macarons and the sophisticated, wood-inspired aesthetic of the brand.

Our process



Key Highlights:
• Eco-Luxurious Material: Premium food-grade kraft with wood-grain textures.
• Artisanal Visuals: Delicate botanical line art reflecting natural ingredients.
• Functional Elegance: Secure internal dividers with a smooth drawer-style opening.

1. Packaging Selection
Focusing on a premium unboxing experience and product safety. We selected a drawer-style structure for a luxury feel, utilizing sustainable food-grade cardstock with a tactile wood-grain finish. Custom-fitted inserts were integrated to prevent macaron breakage during transit.



2. Design Direction
Blending "A Taste of Nature’s Elegance" with modern minimalism. The design utilizes a soft Earth Tone palette to complement vibrant macaron colors, featuring hand-drawn botanical illustrations and a sophisticated mix of elegant script and classic Serif fonts.



3. Mockup Testing & Validation
Bridging the gap between digital concepts and physical products. We utilized 3D visualization to verify color harmony and created physical prototypes to test the slide mechanism, material durability, and the visual impact of Rose Gold foil stamping.



4. Final Layout & Completion
Ensuring perfection from the production line to the customer's hand. This stage included a final quality audit for food safety labels, shelf-ready display design, and the addition of premium touches like hemp twine and wax seals for a complete gift experience.
